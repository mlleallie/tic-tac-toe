Let's play a game...

Welcome to Celebrity Tic-Tac-Toe! The rules of the game are easy:

  1. Why be yourself when you can be someone better? Pick your player!
  2. Make a move (you'll see your celebrity face populate the squares you've claimed)
  3. Wait for your partner to do the same, and then strike again
  4. Whoever gets three in a row(top to bottom, left to right, or diagonally) will be declared the champion!
